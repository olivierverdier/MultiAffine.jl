An affine group modelling matrices of the form

\[œá = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{1} & \mathbf{0} \\ X & g \end{bmatrix}\]

where $g$ is a matrix element of the group $G$, represented in dimension $n$, and $X$ is a $n √ó k$ matrix. If we denote such an element by $[X,g]$, the multiplication law is

MultiAffine.MultiDisplacement ‚Äî Type
MultiDisplacement(n, k=1)

A special case of the MultiAffineGroup group, where the underlying group is the special orthogonal group $SO(n)$. This is just a convenient alias

MultiDisplacement(n, k=1) = MultiAffineGroup(SpecialOrthogonal(n), k)

When $k=1$ (the default), this is the Special Euclidean Group.
